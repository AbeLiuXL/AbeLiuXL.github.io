Biography

Xiaoliang Liu is currently an Associate Professor at the School of Information Engineering, Wenzhou Business College. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Furao Shen. Prior to that, he obtained his M.Sc. degree from the same institution in 2018.

As a core member of the Robotic Intelligence and Neural Computing (RINC) Research Group, his research interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence Security and Computer Vision. He focuses specifically on developing robust deep neural networks and defending against adversarial attacks in open-world environments.
